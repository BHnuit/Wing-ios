Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Welcome to Wing. We understand how important privacy is to you, which is why a "Privacy-First" approach is our core design philosophy. This Privacy Policy explains how Wing handles your data.

1. 100% Local Storage

All your journal entries, images, audio recordings, and settings are stored completely locally on your device (via iOS's secure database). We have no backend servers to store your journal data, and the developer has no way to access, view, or collect your private records.

2. Secure Key Storage (Keychain)

Wing allows you to use third-party AI models for journal synthesis. All API Keys you provide are securely encrypted and stored using Apple's highest-grade security mechanism, the Keychain. This information is never uploaded to any server or shared with other third parties.

3. Third-Party AI Models

When you use AI functionalities such as "Journal Synthesis", necessary journal fragments are temporarily transmitted over an encrypted network to the third-party Large Language Model provider of your choice (e.g., OpenAI, Gemini, DeepSeek).

  • Your data transmission only happens between your device and the destination provider.
  • Please note that when data is sent to a third-party API, the data processing will follow the privacy policy of the respective service provider.
  • We recommend that you review the official privacy policies of your chosen model provider before using their services.

4. Data Collection and Tracking

Wing does not contain any hidden tracking scripts, advertising SDKs, or user behavior analytics tools. We do not collect your usage habits for commercial or advertising purposes. All your interactions within the app are private and entirely under your control.

5. Your Rights

Because all data is stored on your local device:

  • Modification and Deletion: You can modify or permanently delete any journal content within the app at any time. This irreversibly erases the corresponding data from your device.
  • Data Export: You can export and back up all your data in standard formats via the in-app "Data Management" feature, giving you full ownership of your data at all times.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Relevant updates will be published on this page. If there are significant changes, we will notify you within the app.
